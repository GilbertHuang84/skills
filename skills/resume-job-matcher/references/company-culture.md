# Company Culture & Work Environment Assessment

Framework for inferring company culture and identifying community intelligence sources.

## 1. Culture Inference Framework

When direct employee reviews are unavailable, infer culture from observable signals.

### Overtime Culture Assessment

| Signal | Likely Culture | Confidence |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| Startup (<50 people), pre-revenue | High overtime risk (996 or worse) | High |
| Small company, massive hiring (2x headcount) | Overtime likely during transition | Medium |
| Game industry, project-based | Crunch periods around milestones | High |
|外包/代工 company | Driven by client deadlines, overtime common | High |
| Mature company (200+), stable revenue | More predictable hours | Medium |
| AI/tech startup, funded | Variable — depends on product launch pressure | Medium |

### Team Stability Indicators

| Positive Signal | Negative Signal |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Low turnover on LinkedIn/Boss直聘 | Same position reposted frequently |
| Employee tenure >2 years (check LinkedIn) | Multiple recent departures in same role |
| Internal promotion paths mentioned in JD | No growth path visible |
| Reasonable experience requirements (3-5yr) | Unrealistic requirements (10yr for junior pay) |

### Management Style Inference

| Company Type | Likely Style | Risk |
|-------------|-------------|------|
| Founder-led startup | Direct, hands-on, may be micromanaging | Medium |
| Traditional Chinese game company | Hierarchical, top-down | Medium-High |
| International studio (国际知名科技公司) | Collaborative, process-driven | Low |
| AI/tech unicorn (well-funded) | Mix — tech team may be collaborative, business side pressured | Medium |
| Small outsourcing shop | Depends entirely on owner | High |

## 2. Industry-Specific Culture Patterns

### Industry-Specific Patterns

- **Outsourcing companies**: Driven by client deadlines, crunch is norm. Smaller outfits may have more personal flexibility but less stability.
- **Game studios**: Milestone crunch is standard. Better studios offer comp time or bonuses.
- **AI companies**: Fast-paced, often startup culture. May offer equity but high burnout risk.
- **影视/后期制作**: Project-based, employment may be contract-based. Between-project gaps are common.

### Red Flags (General)

- No mention of tools or processes in JD (may mean chaotic process)
- "全能" or "多面手" in requirements (may mean understaffed)
- Salary significantly below market for the role (may mean high turnover)
- No mention of team size or reporting structure

## 3. Community Intelligence Sources

### For Niche/Specialized Roles

These roles often have tight-knit communities where insiders share company information:

| Platform | How to Use | What to Look For |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **微信群/QQ群** | Search "技术交流群", "专业岗位群", "岗位交流群" | Current/former employees sharing experiences |
| **知乎** | Search "[公司名] 加班", "[公司名] 工作体验" | Anonymous employee reviews |
| **脉脉** | Search company name, check employee reviews | Salary data, overtime reports, management reviews |
| **看准网/Kanzhun** | Company reviews section | Overall rating, pros/cons, CEO approval |
| **BOSS直聘** | Check company page reviews tab | Employee reviews (limited but available) |
| **V2EX** | Search company name in job板块 | Tech-focused discussions |
| **小红书** | Search "[公司名] 面试", "[公司名] 体验" | Casual employee sharing, often honest |
| **Bilibili** | Search "[公司名] 工作日常" | Employee vlogs, office environment |

### For High-Volume Roles (General IT, Software)

| Platform | How to Use |
|----------|-----------|
| **脉脉** | Most comprehensive Chinese professional network for company reviews |
| **Glassdoor** | For international companies or foreign-backed startups |
| **牛客网** | For tech roles, interview experiences and company reviews |
| **LeetCode讨论区** | Company-specific interview and culture discussions |

### Search Strategy

When web searching for culture info, use these query patterns:
- `"[公司名] 工作体验" OR "[公司名] 加班" OR "[公司名] 离职"`
- `"[公司名] 脉脉" OR "[公司名] 看准" OR "[公司名] 知乎"`
- `"[公司名] 面试 经验" OR "[公司名] 工作环境"`

**Budget**: 1-2 searches dedicated to culture research, only if general company search didn't surface culture info.

## 4. How to Present in Report

### When Information IS Available

Present specific findings with source attribution:
> "据脉脉员工评价，该公司存在项目期间加班较多的情况，但非常态化996。"

### When Information is UNAVAILABLE

Be honest about the gap and guide the candidate:
> "未查到该公司的工作文化评价。建议面试时重点了解加班频率和项目节奏。以下提供参考问话术。"

### Risk Level Labels

| Label | Meaning | Report Color |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| 低风险 | Sufficient positive signals, no red flags | Green |
| 中风险 | Mixed signals or insufficient data | Yellow |
| 高风险 | Multiple red flags or confirmed negative reviews | Red |
| 待确认 | No data available, cannot assess | Gray |
