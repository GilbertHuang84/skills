# Interview Scripts & Culture Probing Guide

Non-confrontational questions to probe work culture during interviews. Designed to avoid "谈崩" (breaking down the conversation) while gathering genuine intelligence.

## Core Principles

1. **Never ask directly about overtime** — it signals laziness. Instead, ask about workflow and pace.
2. **Frame questions around "understanding the role"** — shows professionalism and genuine interest.
3. **Ask the interviewer about their experience** — people love talking about themselves, and their answers reveal culture.
4. **Use hypothetical scenarios** — "如果遇到X情况，通常怎么处理？" reveals real processes.
5. **Save sensitive questions for the hiring manager** — HR may give scripted answers; the direct manager reveals more.

## Question Categories

### Category 1: Work Pace & Overtime (间接了解加班)

| Question | What It Reveals | When to Ask |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| "这个岗位日常的一天大概是怎么安排的？" | Work rhythm, meeting load, personal time | First round |
| "团队目前在做的项目大概处于什么阶段？" | Project pressure, upcoming crunch | Any round |
| "项目交付前的工作节奏和平时差别大吗？" | Overtime patterns without saying "加班" | Talk with hiring manager |
| "上一个类似项目，团队大概花了多长时间完成？" | Realistic timeline expectations | Technical round |
| "团队现在大概多少人？这个岗位是新增还是替补？" | Team stability, why they're hiring | Any round |
| "入职后大概需要多久能独立负责项目？" | Onboarding quality, team support | First round |

### Category 2: Team & Collaboration (了解团队状态)

| Question | What It Reveals | When to Ask |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| "这个岗位会跟哪些团队协作比较多？" | Cross-team dynamics, communication load | Any round |
| "团队的技术栈是最近选型的还是已经稳定运行一段时间了？" | Tech stability vs chaos | Technical round |
| "之前做这个岗位的同事，现在是在公司内部转岗了吗？" | Turnover signal (if they left company, red flag) | Talk with hiring manager |
| "团队内部有做技术分享或者培训的习惯吗？" | Learning culture, team investment in people | Any round |
| "如果遇到技术方案上的分歧，通常怎么解决？" | Decision-making style, hierarchy | Technical round |

### Category 3: Management & Growth (了解管理风格)

| Question | What It Reveals | When to Ask |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| "您在这个团队待了多久了？最喜欢这里的工作氛围的哪一点？" | Manager's tenure and genuine enthusiasm | Talk with hiring manager |
| "这个岗位的晋升路径大概是怎样的？" | Growth opportunity or dead end | Any round |
| "公司对技术团队的考核方式是怎样的？" | KPI culture vs outcome-based | HR or manager round |
| "如果有技术改进的想法，通常怎么推动落地？" | Bottom-up innovation possible? | Technical round |
| "团队最近在技术上的主要挑战是什么？" | Real problems vs PR answers | Technical round |

### Category 4: Tools & Process (了解工作环境)

| Question | What It Reveals | When to Ask |
|----------|----------------|-------------|
| "目前团队用什么工具做项目管理和任务跟踪？" | Process maturity (Jira/飞书/口头分配) | Technical round |
| "CI/CD和自动化测试覆盖得怎么样？" | Engineering maturity | Technical round |
| "远程办公和弹性工作制方面，团队是怎么安排的？" | Flexibility, trust culture | HR round |
| "入职后配的电脑和开发环境是提前准备好的还是需要自己搭建？" | Onboarding quality, respect for new hires | Any round |

## Scenario-Based Probing (场景式提问)

These are more natural and harder to give scripted answers:

### Scenario 1: Deadline Pressure
> "如果项目突然提前了交付时间，团队一般会怎么调整？是加人、砍需求、还是加班赶工？"

**解读**: 
- "加人" → 有资源意识，相对健康
- "砍需求" → 有产品思维，懂得取舍
- "加班赶工" → 默认加班文化
- "看情况" → 可能没有成熟应对机制

### Scenario 2: Technical Debt
> "如果发现项目里有一些历史遗留的技术债务，但当前项目时间紧，一般会怎么处理？"

**解读**:
- "先上线再重构" → 务实但可能积累债务
- "安排专门时间处理" → 有技术规范意识
- "不归我管" → 职责边界清晰但可能缺乏协作

### Scenario 3: Disagreement
> "如果和产品/策划对技术方案有不同意见，一般怎么推进？"

**解读**:
- "听产品的" → 技术话语权低
- "数据说话" → 成熟的技术文化
- "看谁级别高" → 等级森严

## Red Flag Answers

| Answer Pattern | Red Flag Level | Meaning |
|---------------|---------------|---------|
| "我们这里节奏比较快" | Medium | Likely means frequent overtime |
| "大家都很拼" | Medium-High | Overtime normalized, peer pressure |
| "看项目情况" (repeatedly) | Medium | No standard process, reactive |
| "上一个人做得挺好的，你照着来就行" | High | No room for improvement, maintenance-only role |
| "这个岗位什么都做" | High | Understaffed, unclear scope |
| "我们是个大家庭" | Medium | May blur professional boundaries |
| "弹性工作制" (without details) | Medium | May mean "弹性加班" |

## How to Present in Report

For each company, provide 3-5 recommended questions tailored to the specific information gaps identified during research. Format:

> **建议面试时了解**：
> 1. [具体问题] — 目的：[想了解什么]
> 2. [具体问题] — 目的：[想了解什么]
> 3. [具体问题] — 目的：[想了解什么]

Add a note about timing:
> "建议在技术面或与直属领导沟通时提出这些问题，HR面可能得到标准化的回答。"
